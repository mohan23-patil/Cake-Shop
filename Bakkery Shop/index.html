About Us

Our Story

Welcome to CAKE-SHOP, where we turn simple ingredients into delightful creations. Founded in 2024 by Mohan Patil, our bakery has been a beloved part of the community, serving freshly baked bread, cakes, and pastries with a smile.

Our passion for baking began with a family recipe passed down through generations, and today, we blend tradition with innovation to bring you the best in artisanal baked goods. Each product is made with care and the finest ingredients to ensure quality and taste in every bite.

Our Mission

At CAKE-SHOP, our mission is to provide our customers with exceptional baked goods and a warm, welcoming experience. We believe in using high-quality, locally sourced ingredients to create products that not only taste amazing but also bring joy to every occasion.
